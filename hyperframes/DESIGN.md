# DeepBook Risk Console HyperFrames Design

## Style Prompt

Product-led Sui Overflow pitch film inspired by the supplied reference: 8:5 dark product page, full-screen app moments, bold lower-third narration, kinetic product cards, and explicit proof that the demo runs locally. The video should feel like a founder walking judges through a real product, not like a slide deck.

## Colors

- Canvas: #061014
- Panel: #0d1b20
- Deep panel: #050b0f
- Text: #f5fbff
- Muted text: #9db1bf
- Accent: #b998ff
- Proof green: #42f59f

## Typography

- Primary: Inter, Arial, sans-serif
- Monospace labels: JetBrains Mono, Consolas, monospace
- Use large sentence-case headlines and outlined bilingual subtitles.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not make isolated static cards.
- Do not put the real app inside a tiny decorative frame.
- Do not use generic blue-purple crypto gradients.
- Do not hide the localnet wallet proof.
