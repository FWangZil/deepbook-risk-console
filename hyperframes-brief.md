## Script

DeepBook Risk Console is a Sui Overflow 2026 project for the DeepBook track.

A wallet-connected DeepBookV3 risk console that turns price predictions and LP guardrails into tiny guarded testnet maker orders with on-chain receipts.

The demo flow is: Connect a testnet wallet, Load SUI/DBUSDC risk inputs, Create a guard policy, Create/fund a BalanceManager, Place a guarded maker order, show DeepBook indexer proof, cancel the order, then recover unused SUI from the BalanceManager back to the wallet.

The key metrics to show are Risk decision: PASS, DeepBook order status: placed, GuardReceipt created, and unused-SUI recovery after cancel.
